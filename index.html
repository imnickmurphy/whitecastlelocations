Closed Locations

Wichita, KS

First White Castle location opened in 1921, closed in 1936 when the headquarters moved to Columbus, OH.

Omaha, NE

Closed in 1936 along with Wichita locations as part of a strategic relocation.

Miami, FL

Opened in the late 1950s, closed in 1967 due to supply chain inefficiencies.

Jackson Heights, NY

Opened in 1935, closed in 2021 after 86 years of operation.

Address: 88-08 Northern Blvd, Jackson Heights, NY 11372

New Brunswick, NJ

Closed in January 2021 after 27 years.

Chicago, IL

  • White Castle #16 (43 E. Cermak Rd.): Opened in 1930, this location has been preserved as a Chicago Landmark due to its architectural significance.
  • 79th and Essex: The first White Castle in Chicago, opened in 1929, and has since been demolished.
